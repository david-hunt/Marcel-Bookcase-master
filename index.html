How it Works

  • Overview
  • Borrow a Book
  • Return a Book

Each month, three people from the team can spend up to $100 on a set of books of their choice to add to the bookcase. We only stipulate that it is related to what we do and that you could see it working as a good resource for the team. We welcome a diverse range of subjects: technology, creativity, data, product, startups, research and research papers, magazines, leadership...

  1. Go to marcelbookcase.com
  2. Select borrow a book
  3. Add the SKU of the book from the inside cover
  4. Add your name (as the borrower)
  5. Hit ‘submit’ and you can take the book
  1. Go to marcelbookcase.com
  2. Select return a book
  3. Add the SKU of the book from the inside cover
  4. Add your name
  5. Hit ‘submit’ and place the book back on the same shelf as you found it
